What to bring

Bring what you have. If you are missing something, come anyway.

Your bills
Energy, water, phone, internet or any others.
Letters or notices
Anything you have had in the mail about money you owe.
Concession card
Concession or health care card, if you have one.
Photo ID
Something with your name on it, if you have it.

What happens at an event

Free and private. Take your time.

Step 1
Walk in and say hello
No booking needed. We will help you get started.
Step 2
Meet your provider
Face to face information and advice, one to one — with an interpreter if you need one.
Step 3
Get help there and then
Payment plans, hardship help, and other supports on the day.
Step 4
Leave with a plan
Clear next steps, and someone to follow up if you need it.
Need an interpreter?
Let us know at the door and we will arrange language help.
