# Bring Your Bills — user guides

Plain-English guides for everyone who uses the Bring Your Bills website,
one per audience. Pick the one that matches you:

| Guide | Who it's for |
|---|---|
| [Community members](community-members.md) | Anyone who wants to come along to an event and get help with their bills |
| [SECL staff](secl-staff.md) | South East Community Links staff who run the site day to day — adding events, approving requests, viewing registrations |
| [Providers & partners](providers-partners.md) | Services, councils and community organisations who host events, attend events, or want their logo listed |
| [Interested parties](interested-parties.md) | Funders, researchers, journalists, other community organisations — anyone who just wants to understand what this is |

**The website address** (type this into your browser):

> **bringyourbills.org.au**

`bringyourbills.com.au` and `bringyourbills.au` also work — they take you to
the same site.

Questions about anything in these guides: **byb@secl.org.au**
