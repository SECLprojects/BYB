# Bring Your Bills — guide for providers & partners

For services, councils and community organisations who host Bring Your
Bills events, attend them, or want their logo shown alongside an event.

## What you need first: the partner passcode

Submitting anything on the website needs the **partner passcode** — a
shared code SECL gives to trusted partner organisations. If you don't
have it, email **byb@secl.org.au** and ask.

Everything you submit with it is **checked by SECL staff before it goes
live** — you'll hear back at the email address you give on the form.
Nothing appears on the public calendar until SECL approves it.

## The pages

| What you want | Type this in your browser |
|---|---|
| See the public calendar | `bringyourbills.org.au/calendar.html` |
| See events on a map | `bringyourbills.org.au/map.html` |
| Add / edit / remove an event, confirm attendance, add your logo | `bringyourbills.org.au/request.html` |

## Adding your event to the calendar

1. Go to `bringyourbills.org.au/request.html`.
2. Enter the **partner passcode**, your **name**, your **organisation**,
   and a **contact email** (this is how SECL gets back to you — it's
   never published).
3. Under **"What would you like to do?"** choose **"Add a new event"**.
4. Fill in the event details:
   - **Date**, and **time** written plainly (e.g. `10am to 2pm`).
   - **Title** — e.g. `Bring Your Bills – Dandenong`.
   - **Venue**, and the **street address**. Please include the address —
     it's what makes the "Get directions" button work and puts your
     event's pin on the map automatically.
   - **Region** — pick the area your event is in from the list.
   - **Status** — `Open` if people can come, `Full`, or `Call to
     discuss`.
   - **Host** — your organisation's name.
   - **Event type** — pick "Partnership event" if you're running it with
     SECL, or "Other BYB event" if it's your own.
5. Add anything else SECL should know in the **"Anything else?"** box.
6. Press **Send request**. You're done — SECL will review it and email
   you, and once approved your event appears on the calendar, the map,
   and (if it's the soonest one) the home page.

## Changing or cancelling your event

Same form, same passcode:

1. Choose **"Edit an event"** or **"Remove an event"**.
2. Pick your event from the **"Existing event"** dropdown.
3. For an edit, fill in the details as they should now read.
4. Send — SECL reviews and applies it.

## Getting your logo on event pages

Each event has its own public page listing "Services at this event",
with each organisation's logo. Your logo only needs to be uploaded
**once** — after that it's reused automatically.

1. On the request form, choose **"Add a new service/partner logo"**.
2. Type your organisation's name exactly as you want it displayed.
3. Choose your logo file — **PNG, JPEG or WebP, under 500KB**. A roughly
   square version looks best.
4. Send, and wait for SECL's approval.

## Confirming you're attending someone else's event

If your service will have a table or workers at an event another
organisation is hosting:

1. On the request form, choose **"Confirm attendance at an event"**.
2. Pick the **event**, then pick your **service** from the dropdown.
   - Not in the dropdown yet? Do the logo step above first, wait for
     approval, then come back.
3. Send. Once approved, your name and logo appear on that event's page
   in the alphabetical "Services at this event" list.

## Your details and privacy

Your name, organisation and contact email are used only so SECL can
review your request and follow up — they're never published or shared
beyond that, and they're deleted after 5 years at the latest. You can
ask for a correction or deletion any time at **byb@secl.org.au**. Full
policy: secl.org.au/policies.

## Questions?

**byb@secl.org.au** — for the passcode, help with the form, or anything
about running an event with SECL.
